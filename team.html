About Nova Barber Co.

Nick Finks is the founder of Nova Barber Co., a modern barbershop located in Folsom, California. With over 19 years of experience behind the chair, Nick has built a reputation for precision haircuts, consistency, and a high-level client experience.

At Nova Barber Co., the focus is simple: deliver clean, detailed cuts in a professional, elevated environment.

The shop brings together a team of skilled barbers dedicated to quality, culture, and service — making it one of the go-to barbershops in Folsom.

Whether you're looking for a fade, taper, beard work, or a complete transformation, Nova Barber Co. sets the standard for modern grooming in the Folsom and Sacramento area.
